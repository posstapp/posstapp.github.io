Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Effective: May 2026

posst.app is a social media automation service based in Australia. We take privacy seriously and only collect what we need to deliver the service.

1. Who We Are

posst.app ("we", "our", "us") is a social media management service that automates posting to Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile on behalf of our clients. We are based in Australia.

Contact: chief@posst.app

2. What Information We Collect

When you sign up for posst.app, we collect:

  • Name and email address — to identify your account and send service communications
  • Business details — business name, location, type, description, and brand keywords to generate content for your accounts
  • Phone number — for account verification and support
  • Facebook and Instagram access tokens — obtained via Meta OAuth to post on your behalf
  • Google access and refresh tokens — obtained via Google OAuth to manage your Google Business Profile
  • Social media account identifiers — Page IDs, Business Account IDs, and GBP Location IDs needed to deliver posts

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to deliver the posst.app service:

  • Generating and scheduling social media posts on your connected accounts
  • Responding to Google Business Profile reviews on your behalf
  • Sending service emails (onboarding, go-live confirmation, monthly reports)
  • Diagnosing and fixing technical issues with your account

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to deliver the service (see Section 5).

4. Meta Platform Data

posst.app accesses Facebook and Instagram data through the Meta Platform. We use this access only to:

  • Post content to your Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts
  • Read basic page information needed to confirm posts were published

We do not store your followers' data. We do not scrape or resell any Meta platform data. Your Meta access tokens are stored securely and used only for the above purposes.

Our use of Meta data complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Meta Developer Policies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate posst.app:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — to publish posts via their API
  • Google — to manage Google Business Profile via their API
  • OpenAI — to generate post captions. Your business details are sent to OpenAI for this purpose
  • Cloudflare — for media storage
  • Vultr — for server infrastructure (data hosted in Australia/US)
  • Google Sheets — as an internal data store for client account information

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. Access tokens are stored in encrypted form. We limit access to your data to only the systems and personnel required to deliver the service.

We retain your data for the duration of your subscription and for up to 90 days after cancellation, after which it is deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (we will action this within 30 days)
  • Revoke access to your Facebook, Instagram, or Google accounts at any time via those platforms' settings

To exercise any of these rights, email chief@posst.app.

8. Cookies

posst.app's public website (posst.app) does not use tracking cookies. We may use essential cookies for the onboarding flow only.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify active clients by email of any material changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at chief@posst.app.
